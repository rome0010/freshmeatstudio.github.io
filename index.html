My Projects

Myth Defender

Myth Defender is a 2D platform action-defense game where you protect your castle from waves of mythical creatures drawn from Bicol folklore. Armed with a magical hook, you must pull and defeat enemies before they reach your base.

Castle of Secret

Castle of Secrets is a mobile card guessing game with 5 cards per row. Your goal is to find and guess the locations of the two special cards: the Key and the Door, in order to move to the next row above

Hook Fireee

Fire Hook is a fast-paced 2D Topdown Multiplayer Action Game where players face off using grappling hooks to pull, dodge, and outplay each other. In a top-down arena filled with hazards like lava and platforms, one well-timed hook can decide the match.

Saving Princess

Saving the Princess is a 3D action adventure game where you use a grappling hook to swing across towering buildings while being chased by armed robot enemies.
